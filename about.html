BLUE HAZE Our first creation full of authenticity and Love.

The motive behind BLUE HAZE's creation was care,love for people. To serve tasty yet favorable foods. To see families,friends enjoy and create memories. It was in 2022 , when we suddenly decided one night to create this. To show world, hope can lead you to anywhere. With over 2 franchies, we are here near you to fall in love with food again! . Not only this, but we also started our own delivery options nearly one year ago available 24/7 to be comfortable. In last,we create love and believe in sharing.Thus,we do expect it in return.Therefore,your feedbacks help us grow and learn. Below are some dishes we love to serve and our bestsellers till date.

Smoothie!Love of our Lives.

"The right mix of ingredients can help you achieve your nutrition goals".We're all busy! If done the right way, smoothies can be an easy option for a healthy snack or a meal replacement in a pinch.Our restaurant mainly focusses on health of our customers and therefore making it essential for us to make them satisfied and happy.

Dal Rice!India's Everyday meal yet Interesting.

When we hear ‘Dal Rice’ the first word which comes to our mind is ‘comfort food’.Comfort food is food that provides a nostalgic or sentimental value to someone, and may be characterized by its high caloric nature, high carbohydrate level, or it may be a simple preparation. The nostalgia may be specific to an individual, or it may apply to a specific culture.Having originated in India, we surely serve all types of Indfian food,but dal rice is one of our favourite dish,and we tend to love and serve with all our hearts.

Spaghetti!Fun fact: It was discovered in America.

One of the most important times in the history of the spaghetti was World War II. It was then that American soldiers came in close contact with European cuisine, bringing home tales about spaghetti and demanding its presence on the American market. And thus, here we are with dishes all around gthe world,but spaghetti is the one which is most ordered and we tend to make it will all love and care.

Cake!The shine of auspicious day.

We believe cakes are ordered only to celebrate once auspicious day. And thus we focus on to make their day more special with are authentic,freshly baked cake with love to mark their important day with our care and joy. We also believe that baking cake can not only make us happy but the smile we get,the love we get after every order motivates us to make others smile and their eyes shine.
